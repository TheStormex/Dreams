The Last Hacktivists 2

Oh no! This next security program has double the defensive forces!
Don't worry, Nuts and Bolt! Screw and Robot are here to help!
We'll get through together and defeat the Mega Corp!

Instructions:
Defeat all 4 enemies! the fight alternate between Tactics Phase and Combat Phase!


Tactics Phase:
Battlefield is on top of the screen, the actions menu is on the bottom
On the battlefield, Player characters are on the bottom, enemies are on the top
On some turns, enemies will use talents, mouse over them to see what they do
Mouse over player characters to bring up their actions menu
Mouse over the "C" to see that character's combat abilities
Click on abilities to use them, abilities can only be used once per turn (unless renewed) and cost energy
Using abilities gain Ultimate charge, at 100%, they can be used
Click on player characters to change the active Frontline character that will fight this turn

Combat Phase:
The Frontline character will fight. WASD to move, aim and shoot Basic Attack with the mouse
CTRL, SHIFT, and SPACE to choose ability, aim and click to use with the mouse
Combat abilities have cooldowns. Each character have 2 ultimate abilities, one for the Tactics Phase and one for Combat Phase
Dodge enemies and their bullets, hitting enemies with bullets gains increases Ultimate charge
At the start of the new turn, each player character gets Energy and enemies may use Talents to buff themselves or disrupt players
