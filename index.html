Dreams Testing

Test out how to code the RPG system

Instructions for testers:
Defeat all enemies. the fight alternate between Plan Phase and Combat Phase


Tactics Phase:
Battlefield on top of screen, actions menu on bottom
On battlefield, Player characters are on the bottom, enemies on the top
Mouse over player characters to bring up their actions menu
Click on abilities to use them, abilities can only be used once per turn (unless renewed) and cost energy
Using abilities gain Ultimate charge, at 100%, it can be used
Click on player characters to change Frontline character

Combat Phase:
The Frontline character will fight. WASD to move, aim and shoot Basic Attack with mouse
CTRL, SHIFT, and SPACE to choose ability, aim and use with mouse
Combat abilities have cooldowns. Each character have 2 ultimate abilities, one for Plan Phase and one for Combat Phase
Dodge enemies and their bullets, hitting enemies gains Ultimate charge as well
At the start of the new turn, each player character gets Energy and enemies may use Talents to buff selves or disrupt players
